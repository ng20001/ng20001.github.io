Site Purpose

This site is to provide audience instant weather information, specifically weather of the city Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. Weather information such as Current temperature, Weather forecast of the week, chance of rain, precipitation, etc., will be included. It also provides immediate Storm information and updates, including possible storm warnings. The site will also act as a platform to present photos of different weather and any astronomical phenomenon.
