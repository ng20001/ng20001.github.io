Target Audience

This site's target audience will be people from all age group who are seeking weather information such as weather forecast in Idaho, audience who seek instant storm updates, and audiences who are interested in viewing pictures of some weather phenomenon.

Two Personas

Peter

Job: Professor

Age: 62 years old

Father of 6 children

Ph.D. in Music Performance

Character: Quiet, strict, believes in perfectionism. Expect things to go the way he expects.

Master at using technology, such as mobile device and computers. A frequent computer user.

Amy

Job: House wife

Age: 42 years old

Mother of 1 child

High school graduate

Character: Outgoing, loves outdoor activities. Easy going and friendly with others.

Beginner on technology or electronic devices. Recently bought a smart phone to communicate with husband using text messenger.
