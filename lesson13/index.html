About us

We are a group of hard working and enthusiastic people who are keen on bringing prosperities to Hong Kong.

We expand members local and international networks with businesses, governments and institutions around the world.

We assist you by broadening your international network by meeting overseas and Mainland business people, as well as participating in our overseas business tours to cities in China and around the world.
